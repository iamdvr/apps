SreeM OM Sivaaya guravE namaH OM OM
--------------------------
1) OM bhoorbhuvaH ssuvaH
   thathsavithurvarENyaM bhargO dhEvasya Dhimahi
   DhiyO yOnaH prachOdhayaath

2) Sivaaya guravE namaH OM OM

3) OM aiM kleeM sauH sauH kleeM aiM

4) OM padhma kairava maMdhaara sumaalinyai namO namaH

5) OM aiM kleeM SreeM kRshNaaya gOviMdhaaya namaH
   OM kleeM SreeM suthaH pradhaaya kRshNaaya namaH

6) OM SaravaNabhavaH
